# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

*Last Updated / 最后更新: 2026-02-22*

---

## Part 1: English Version

This application is developed by an individual developer. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children aged 4 and above.

### 1. Current Offline Status
The current version of this application is a **fully offline game**. 
- **No Data Collection**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information (such as name, email, device ID, or location).
- **No Third-Party SDKs**: This app does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services.
- **Local Storage Only**: All game progress and high scores are saved strictly on the user's local device.

### 2. Future Features (Optional)
In future updates, we may introduce an online leaderboard. 
- These features will be **optional (opt-in)**.
- We will only collect non-identifiable nicknames and game scores provided by the user.
- This Privacy Policy will be updated before such features are officially launched.

### 3. Children's Privacy
This app is designed for users aged 4 and above and complies with children's privacy standards:
- No social networking, chat, or profile features.
- No personal data collection from children.
- No deceptive links or in-app purchases.

### 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions, please contact:
- **Email:** desoxydate@gmail.com
